Your finances

Child Benefit Loans are assessed using Open Banking. Open Banking allows applicants to share financial information with the credit union so that decisions on lending can be made quickly and remotely without effecting your credit rating.

Open Banking is regulated and uses rigorously tested software and security systems to keep your data safe. Open Banking provides read only access and we will use this information to understand your financial position and confirm your receipt of child benefit.

Agreeing to the use of Open Banking means we are able to give you an instant decision on your loan application.

Learn more about Open Banking
Do you agree to the use of Open Banking?

Once submitted you will be redirected to enable open banking.

Please note – your application will need to be reviewed and considered by a Loan Officer and you will not receive an immediate decision. Once submitted you will be asked to upload a complete bank statement from the last 30 days. This must show your name, address, bank account details and include your Child Benefit payment.
